HOW TO PLAY

1. Enter your ETH bet and click "Place Bet".

2. Click any cell on the grid to place your orb.

3. A laser fires along a random row or column.

4. If the laser hits your orb — BUST! You lose your bet.

5. If you survive, the row/column is removed. Your multiplier increases.

6. Choose: CASH OUT to collect winnings, or pick a new cell on the shrinking grid for a higher multiplier.

The grid shrinks each round. Higher risk = higher reward!
